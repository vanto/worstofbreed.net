# Analysis
The attempt to force agility into a hierarchical company with bureaucratic violence. Waterfall project management wrapped in new buzzwords.

**The Reality:**
You now have more meetings, more roles, and more complex diagrams, but you deliver software just as slowly as before. Team autonomy is overrun by the "Agile Release Train" (ART).
